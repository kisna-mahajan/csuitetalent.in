Our Partnership Process

How It Works

Our engagement model is structured to be clean, professional, and completely secure for both you and your client.

1

Secure B2B Agreement

We sign a comprehensive Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and a clear, legally binding Revenue-Sharing Contract to ensure your financial and relationship interests are fully protected before any client details are shared.

2

Introduce the Mandate

You bridge the initial relationship connection between C-Suite Talent and the hiring client's leadership team regarding their executive position vacancy. Your involvement is complete at this stage.

3

Shared Success Payout

C-Suite Talent fully executes, sources, and places the candidate. Upon successful closure and client collection, your agreed percentage fee split is processed.

Fee split paid within 7 working days of collection from the client introduced by you.

Our Commitment

End-to-End Confidentiality Ensured

At the executive level, trust is everything. We understand your client relationships and boardroom access have been built over decades of impeccable professional conduct. Our operational framework is engineered to protect that trust at every stage.
