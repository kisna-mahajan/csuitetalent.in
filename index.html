Our Philosophy

Good vs. Appropriate

A resume tells you how good a candidate is. It rarely tells you whether they are the right leader for your specific challenge. We assess leadership style, problem-solving orientation, and decision-making under pressure — because organisations don't hire experience, they hire solutions.

Good is desirable. Appropriate is transformational.
