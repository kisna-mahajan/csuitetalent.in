Client Recognition

Recognition That Reflects Trust, Performance and Long-Term Partnerships

A proven track record in international executive search — recognised formally by senior client leadership following successful delivery of a landmark cross-border assignment.

Anil Mahajane, MBA (IIFT), receiving client recognition from Mr. Arun Kumar Dixit, Projects Director & Technical Development Advisor, and Mr. Salah M. Hakim, Head of Human Resource Management, at the Taj Mahal Palace Hotel, Mumbai.

The Assignment

A Proven Track Record in International Executive Search Leadership

In 2010, Anil Mahajane led a significant international executive search programme for one of Saudi Arabia's leading publicly listed cement companies.

The engagement involved identifying and recruiting senior leadership talent across multiple international markets. The assignment focused on sourcing highly experienced professionals for critical leadership positions spanning plant operations, manufacturing, engineering, technical services and senior management. Candidates were identified from some of the world's most respected cement organisations, including Lafarge, Holcim and other leading global manufacturers.

Successfully executing such a large-scale, cross-border executive search programme required extensive market intelligence, leadership assessment, international talent mapping and close collaboration with senior client leadership.
